Project Name

The Fauxica Home Page is a sleek and modern e-selling platform designed for showcasing and selling products like clothes, shoes, jackets, and more. The project highlights engaging user experiences with animations and hover interactions, making the interface dynamic and interactive. Utilizing Figma's powerful design tools, I crafted frames and components to ensure consistency and scalability throughout the design. The project emphasizes a user-friendly layout, visually appealing elements, and a seamless navigation flow to enhance the shopping experience for users.
