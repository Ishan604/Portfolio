EVC Mobile Streaming Platform


The eVideo Cloud Mobile App is a comprehensive cloud-based video platform designed to cater to both video producers and consumers. It features a user-centric interface emphasizing accessibility and responsiveness across devices. The app is organized into key modules, including the EPN for producers to manage content and finances, eVideo Discovery for consumers to search and browse videos with advanced filters, and My Library for organizing and interacting with purchased or rented content. The integrated eM Player provides intuitive playback controls, playlist management, and non-intrusive video suggestions, while the Settings module allows customization of appearance, language, payment methods, and device management. With a streamlined navigation structure, enhanced UI/UX elements, secure login mechanisms, and performance optimization, EVC ensures a seamless and engaging experience for its users.
