A Simple Portfolio Design

The Simple Portfolio Design in Figma is a clean and modern showcase of creativity and professionalism. It features a well-structured navigation bar with essential sections such as Contact Me, About Me, and Projects for easy access. The design incorporates subtle animations using frames and components, adding interactivity and a polished feel to the user experience. With a focus on simplicity and elegance, the portfolio highlights both visual appeal and functionality, making it an excellent platform to present skills, projects, and personal branding effectively.
